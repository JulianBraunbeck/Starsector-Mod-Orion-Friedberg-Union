Location: Outside Altho Local Patrol Volume
Date: 177.01.26

Sweeping sensor pings have identified an unregistered fleet hiding in a nearby nebula, just off the fringe jump point. Ops believes the contact arrived several days ago and has since remained concealed.

Available intelligence suggests its target is a pair of lightly protected mercantile convoys due to arrive within the next three days.

Despite the enemy’s apparent numerical advantage, Captain Halding has ordered his squadron to battle stations—confident in their ships, and in the training that brought them this far. in their ships and training in the face of the pirate scrap fleet.
